# TeamOfThePing_F2025

A custom modpack designed for the **TeamOfThePing** Valheim server. This pack focuses on quality-of-life improvements, naval construction, and profession systems.

## Included Features
* **Quality of Life:** Quick stacking, sorting, trash options, and **ExtraSlots** for better inventory management.
* **Building & Crafting:** Includes **OdinArchitect** for expanded structural pieces and **VNEI** to easily look up recipes and item drops.
* **Sailing & Exploration:** Experience the **ValheimRAFT** system, allowing you to build custom ships from the ground up, supported by a dedicated **Shipyard** and Smoothbrain's **Sailing** skills.
* **Agriculture & Gathering:** **PlantEverything** and **PlantEasily** for farming, plus dedicated **Mining**, **Farming**, and **Cooking** skill trees.
* **Multiplayer Tools:** **ServerSideMap** and **MultiUserChest** for seamless coordination and shared storage with friends.
